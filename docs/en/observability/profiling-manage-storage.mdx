<div id="profiling-manage-storage"></div>

Universal Profiling provides the following ways to manage how your data is stored.

* <DocLink slug="/en/observability/profiling-index-lifecycle-management">Index lifecycle management</DocLink> automatically manages your indices according to age or size metric thresholds. Universal Profiling ships with a default index lifecycle policy, but you can create a custom policy to meet your requirements.
* <DocLink slug="/en/observability/profiling-probabilistic-profiling">Probabilistic profiling</DocLink> mode uses representative samples of profiling data to reduce storage needs even further.
