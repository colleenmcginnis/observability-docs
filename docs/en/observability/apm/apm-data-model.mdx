<div id="apm-data-model"></div>

Elastic APM agents capture different types of information from within their instrumented applications.
These are known as events, and can be `spans`, `transactions`, `errors`, or `metrics`.

* <DocLink id="enObservabilityApmDataModelSpans">Spans</DocLink>
* <DocLink id="enObservabilityApmDataModelTransactions">Transactions</DocLink>
* <DocLink id="enObservabilityApmDataModelErrors">Errors</DocLink>
* <DocLink id="enObservabilityApmDataModelMetrics">Metrics</DocLink>

Events can contain additional <DocLink id="enObservabilityApmDataModelMetadata">metadata</DocLink> which further enriches your data.
