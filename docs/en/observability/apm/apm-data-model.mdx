<div id="apm-data-model"></div>

Elastic APM agents capture different types of information from within their instrumented applications.
These are known as events, and can be `spans`, `transactions`, `errors`, or `metrics`.

* <DocLink slug="/en/observability/apm-data-model-spans">Spans</DocLink>
* <DocLink slug="/en/observability/apm-data-model-transactions">Transactions</DocLink>
* <DocLink slug="/en/observability/apm-data-model-errors">Errors</DocLink>
* <DocLink slug="/en/observability/apm-data-model-metrics">Metrics</DocLink>

Events can contain additional <DocLink slug="/en/observability/apm-data-model-metadata">metadata</DocLink> which further enriches your data.
